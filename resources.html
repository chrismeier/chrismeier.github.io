Books

Rework by 37 Signals

Interesting read although they aim for universal truths and I didn’t find it to be entirely applicable, neverthless some good life lessons and their ties to business are represenested well.

The Lean Startup by Eric Ries

An overview of the lean methodology that is worth reading if you are interested in start-ups.

Running Lean by Ash Maurya

Follows The Lean Startup and is heavily focused on the business plan (Lean Canvas in this case) and getting your project from an idea to a potential business. This includes identifying a problem/solution fit, validating it through customer interviews, identfiying a product/market fit, and then tuning that fit to see growth.
