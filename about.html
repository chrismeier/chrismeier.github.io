About Me

I am an almost college grad coming out of California State University, Chico with a major in Management Information Systems. I became interested in learning Ruby on Rails during the summer of 2012 and through the next year I slowly played around with it. This Blog began the following summer (June 2013) and is a means to discuss my understanding of Ruby on Rails and document my learning of it. I imagine the first dozen or so posts are incredibly boring and the over time will discuss more enhanced topics; as I am learning from the beginning that is just the nature of the beast. I have discussed my previous experience in a blog post here.

So if you are just beginning to learn Ruby on Rails my goal is to offer you a resource for pitfalls I encountered so hopefully you can avoid them. I also intend to understand those mistakes and pass on the knowledge learned for those that want to understand why it occured in the first place.

By proxy blogging about my experiences with Ruby on Rails I can document my knowledge of the language and better commit it to memory. If you are learning to I would highly reccomend blogging as you go too. Although I haven’t shared much at the time of writing it has forced me to critically think about what I did and to be sure of my solution.

If you have read this far or any of my posts I thank you for taking the time out of your day and I encourage you to comment on posts if you are interested in discussing the topic further (or telling me how wrong I am).
